# Medicine Scheduling App - Screen List & Requirements

## Phase 1: Entry & Onboarding
1. **Splash Screen**: Minimalist branding with an animated logo feel.
2. **Auth (Login/Signup)**: Clean, simple forms with social login options.
3. **Onboarding (9 Questions)**: A step-by-step flow to personalize the experience.
    - Q1: Name/Profile setup.
    - Q2: Primary health goal (e.g., 'Never miss a dose', 'Track chronic condition').
    - Q3: Types of medications (Pills, Injections, Liquids).
    - Q4: Frequency of intake.
    - Q5: Notification preference.
    - Q6: Current health status/conditions.
    - Q7: Primary caregiver/Emergency contact.
    - Q8: Pharmacy preference.
    - Q9: Review & Confirm.
4. **Paywall**: Value-driven premium features (Smart adaptive alerts, family sharing) with clear CTA.

## Phase 2: Core Experience
5. **Dashboard (Home)**: Daily timeline of doses, progress gauges, and 'Quick Log' actions.
6. **Schedule**: Detailed calendar view with vertical timeline (inspired by reference image).
7. **Medication Library**: List of current meds with stock levels.
8. **Add Medication**: Multi-step form to add new meds with dosage and timing.
9. **Insights/Reports**: Adherence tracking and health trends.
10. **Profile/Settings**: Personal info, notification logic, and account management.
